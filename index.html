A little bit about us...

The Colour Bar for Hair is Mannings most reliable local hair salon. Our salon has been running since 2015 and have developed a strong local client base who we love and adore. The Colour Bar is owned by Vicki Arkingstall our senior hairdresser and stylist with over 20 years of experience. Our salon can provide you with whatever you are looking for with skills in colour, foils, styling, cutting and various treatments on offer to new and existing clients. The salon is located in the manning shops towards the end.

We are a close knit team with a strong passion for ensuring our clients leave the salon with their dream hair, a big smile and as much extra confidence as we can offer them. The Colour Bar for Hair is a Wella salon which means we stock all of the brands products as well as use them in all our services. All the team members can provide you with strong reccomendations based on your hairs needs. Click the book now link to secure a spot at the Colour Bar, we are so excited to see you!

Service Menu

All the prices which are displayed in the service menu are base prices. Services are subject to price changes based on hair types, events and time taken etc. All services are accompanied with complimentary shampoo and conditioner as well as tea and coffee in salon.
